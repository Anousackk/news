Agriculture in Laos

Agriculture plays a major role in Laos' economy, this sector contributes about 51% of the GDP. However, Lao agriculture has not play a major role in the country's foreign trade. Agricultural products of Laos are seen with low quality because of a scattered farming, low farm infrastructures and technology and lack of quality control. Lao agriculture also experiences slow growth rate, which has grown only about 3% over the two decades.
